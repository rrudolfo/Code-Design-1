Beef & Broccoli

Ingredients

Only three ingredients so you have no reason not to try this today!

  • Ground Beef 93% 7% - 1 lbs
  • White Rain - 1 cup
  • Broccoli Or any veggie - 170g

Instructions

This recipe can be done in under 30 minutes!

  1. Put 1 cup of white rice into a rice cooker and 1 cup of water
    Make sure to wash your rice!
  2. Put 1 tbsp of olive oil in a medium heat pan
  3. Put ground beef into pan and break it up into desired chunks
  4. Add seasoning of choice, I prefer taco season.
  5. Cook until no pink is left in the meat
  6. Once finished quickly rinse pan with water
  7. Apply broccoli to the same pan and drizzle olive oil
  8. Add a little water and cover to soften
  9. Once finished cooking, remove from pan.
  10. Mix all of your ingredients and despirse into 3 containers
